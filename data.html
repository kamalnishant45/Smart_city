Basic Data and Statistics

Total National CO₂ Emissions: ~2.88 billion metric tons (2021) Contribution by Cities: Urban areas are responsible for nearly 50% of India's emissions. Major Sectors: Transport (major contributor in cities) Energy sector (coal-heavy) Industry (cement, steel, and chemical industries) Residential energy use (increasing demand for electricity in urban homes)

Key insights:

    Transportation is the largest source of emissions in most Indian cities due to the growing number of vehicles.
    Electricity generation, particularly from coal, contributes significantly in cities like Chennai and Kolkata.
    Industrial activities play a major role, especially in cities like Mumbai, Chennai, and Delhi.
    Many cities have started initiatives to reduce CO₂ emissions through renewable energy adoption and public transport improvements, but progress is gradual.
