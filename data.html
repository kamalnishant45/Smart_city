Green Smart City: Data and Statistics

India's total CO₂ emissions reached ~2.88 billion metric tons in 2021. Urban areas contribute nearly 50% of these emissions.

Key Insights:

  • Transportation is the largest source of emissions in most Indian cities.
  • Coal-based electricity generation contributes significantly in cities like Chennai and Kolkata.
  • Industrial activities in Mumbai, Chennai, and Delhi are major contributors to urban emissions.
  • Cities are slowly adopting renewable energy and public transport improvements to reduce CO₂ emissions.
